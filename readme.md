## cutthroat

---
A tool that finds out if a given image is AI generated or edited by AI or not
---

The idea is to make a cli c/c++ tool, to find out if a given image is generated/edited by AI or not?
The constraint is to do it without embedding AI in our tool, just using pure mathematical algorithms.
